Current behavior

Basic Sync Behavior

  • Interested and qualified opportunities may be created in or linked to Salesforce.
  • Existing records are matched using the configured external-source identifier.
  • Source updates for linked opportunities may be pushed outbound to Salesforce.
  • BidLens does not currently perform general bidirectional synchronization.
